What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
an ip address is identifying numbers used for any computer- servers included. A DNS is a way for 
websites to have easily memorable names (human-friendly) that can be reverted into a IP addresses
(computer-friendly), so it is easier surf the web. It's like a phonebook, or the contacts on your phone;
Scrolling through contacts, to call a friend you would click on "John", whereas the phone translates this to
"John's phone number" and calls accordingly.

What is a cookie?
Cookies are bits of data that are saved by a server to identify specific computers and information
that is related to specific computers; like logins and frequently visited pages.
